In Lomonosov, in the suburbs on Saint-Petersburg, there is a sand mine Krasnoflotskoye, that provides the local market with high quality sand: sandy soil, grained sand and inwash sand. You can order sand of those types in three different grain sizes (large, medium and small) in any volume with delivery or pick it up yourself at Baltic Sand.


Types of sand


Sand we provide has a slightly yellow color and it comes in three grain sizes: small (the size of each grain is less than 0,02 sm), medium (0,03 sm) and large (0,05 sm).

Characteristics and advantages of quarry sand

  • Correspondence to the weight of the density of sand: in the presence of excess moisture, the density increases simultaneously with an increase in grain size - this negatively affects the quality of the resource.
  • The ratio of the volume of sand to its mass coefficient (specific gravity index).
  • The presence of impurities: in raw sand, the percentage is 10, after processing it is reduced to 0.3%.
  • The humidity parameters according to the standards should not exceed 7%
  • Radioactive levels: our company provides sand with low radioactivity (Grade 1), confirmed by certificates.

Classification of sand by degree of purification

Depending on the type of processing, sand that is provided from Krasnoflotskoye sand mine can be divided into three categories:

  1. Sandy soil- unprocessed, does not go through any purification techniques. It is an affordable resource price-wise and is widely used in road construction and aligning relief.
  2. Grained sand - sifting is carried out in the quarry by the technical-mechanical method, which frees the mound from stones and other large components. It is more expensive than the previous type, but you can use it for many more purposes: from manufacturing plaster mortars to formating foundations.
  3. Inwash sand - our sand mine has high-tech hydromechanical equipment that is used for this type of sand. After cleaning, the material is clean and free from impurities. Such a resource is used in the production of bricks, foam blocks, concrete structures and in the construction of the residential sector.
